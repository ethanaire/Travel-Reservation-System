List of SQL Queries: 

  1. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) - Identify the most valuable customers.
  2. Most Popular Flight Routes - Determine which airport pairs are most commonly traveled.
  3. Seasonal Travel Demand - Analyze how flight bookings fluctuate throughout the year. 
  4. Aircraft Utilization - Find which aircraft types are most commonly used.
  5. Hotel Ratings by City - Determine where high-rated hotels are concentrated.
  6. Impact of Breakfast Inclusion on Hotel Booking Price - Evaluate how breakfast impacts hotel pricing.
  7. Frequent Travelers - Identify repeat travelers who book multiple trips.
