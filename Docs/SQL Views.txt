List of SQL Views: 

- Customer Insight Views: 
  1. Total spending by top individual customers
  2. Customer Demographics
  3. Customers by country
  4. Customer age distribution
  5. Top customers by spending 
  6. Returning vs new customers

- Travel Information Views: 
  1. Aggregated flight bookings by airline, route, and class
  2. Frequent and high-revenue flight routes
  3. Hotel check-in/check-out trends and revenue
  4. Route demand
  5. Booking mix 

- Operational Information Views: 
  1. Airline usage frequency
  2. Aircraft types per airline
  3. Top airports by the number of departures and arrivals
  4. Airline and airport combinations
